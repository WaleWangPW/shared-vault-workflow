# Resources Checklist

Use this checklist before adding anything into `Resources/` or publishing a reusable template.

## Good candidates

- blank templates
- naming conventions
- checklists
- generic examples
- workflow diagrams
- non-sensitive reference material

## Keep out of Resources

- live credentials
- private exports
- personal notes
- personal screenshots containing names or account details
- machine-specific config
- files copied from `~/.claude`, `~/.openclaw`, or other local config folders

## Before adding a file

Check that it:

- works without your private account
- works without your private machine name
- works without your current absolute path
- does not reveal a real teammate, customer, or inbox
- does not require a secret to understand

## Recommended placeholder style

Use neutral placeholders:

- `YOUR_NAME`
- `YOUR_EMAIL`
- `YOUR_DEVICE`
- `YOUR_VAULT_ROOT`
- `YOUR_TEAM`

## If you are unsure

Prefer not to publish the file.
Instead, keep a private version locally and export a redacted copy later.
